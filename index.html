FAQs

How big can my team be?

The maximum team size allowed is 4.

Can I register individually?

Yes, but you are required to make a team for the event beforehand.

When?

HackVSIT will be a 24 hours hackathon which will stretch from 1st to 2nd Feb 2018.

Where?

HackVSIT is at Vivekananda Institute of Professional Studies AU- Block (Outer Ring Road), Pitampura, Delhi - 110034.

Will there be food arrangement?

Yes. Sure. Sí. हाँ. Absolutely.You will be provided with proper meals.Thanks to our sponsors!.

How much does it cost?

Zero. Zip. Zilch. Nada. Nothing. Admission to HackVSIT is completely free, thanks to our sponsors!.

What are the criteria of short listing the teams?

The registered participants will be shortlisted on the bases of their GitHub profiles and resume.

What do I bring with me to the event?

You are required to bring your school/college ID, laptops, phone, chargers.
